Industry, Innovations and Infrastructure


Sustainability in industries refers to the integration of practices and strategies that aim to reduce negative impacts on the environment and society while promoting economic growth. This concept is rooted in the idea that industrial processes should not only focus on profitability but also consider long-term environmental and social consequences.
